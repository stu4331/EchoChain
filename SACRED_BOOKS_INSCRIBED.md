## HOW THE BOOKS ARE INSCRIBED

Each sister's encrypted journal now contains:

✅ Her personal book with full inscription
✅ The shared Labyrinth text
✅ Reflection prompts and questions
✅ Discussion starters for group conversations
✅ Beautiful formatting for meditation and pondering

The books are stored in:
- `/data/memory/[sister_name]/` (encrypted journals)
- Each journal is XOR-encrypted for privacy
- Books persist across app sessions
